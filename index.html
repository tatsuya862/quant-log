Philosophy

QUANT LOG is not a place to chase certainty. Markets, decisions, and life itself are built on probability, not promises. Risk does not disappear by ignoring it — it only becomes invisible.

This project exists to make risk explicit. Through numbers, logs, and continuous observation, I attempt to separate structure from noise, and process from outcome.

Results are temporary. Records remain. What matters is not being right once, but understanding why something worked — or failed — and whether it can be reproduced.

No signals. No guarantees. Only documented thinking and measured exposure.

What is QUANT LOG?

QUANT — Structure, probability, and numerical thinking.

LOG — Records, verification, and reproducibility.

LIFE — Markets and decision-making share the same principles.
